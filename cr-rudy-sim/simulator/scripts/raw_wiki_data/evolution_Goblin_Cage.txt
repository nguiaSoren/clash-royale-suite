## Release Date

The Goblin Cage's Evolution is aRarecardthat is unlocked with 6 Evolution Shards. It spawns a Goblin Cage with identical stats to the original. Once a ground troop comes within 3 tiles of the building, they get pulled into the cage and they are attacked directly by the Goblin Cage, and any troop pulled will be freed. When defeated, it spawns a Goblin Brawler with identical stats to the original. It appears to be a wooden cage with four dark violet metal supports with a golden spike on them, a lock on the front with theCard Evolutionsymbol on it, and a pair of bars at each side, while the Goblin Brawler appears as a large, bulkyGoblinwith violet eyes, mohawk and unibrow with a golden piercing, has tattoos printed on his back and arms, and sports a striped white shirt and a gold chain with the Card Evolution symbol, along with violet pants and bands tied on his hands which extends till his lower arms. The Goblin Cage’s Evolution costs 4Elixirand requires 1 cycle to deploy.

## Strategy[]

  - When the Evolved Goblin Cage pulls a unit into the cage, it won't be targeted by other units. This is especially helpful at separating win conditions, likeGiantorGolemfrom their support troops, likeWitchorNight Witch, to make defending easier.
  - If a troop is already on the Crown Tower, the user can deploy the Evolved Goblin Cage next to them to pull them into the cage. This can result in the Crown Tower taking significantly less damage if done properly.Done to theGiant Skeleton, the death bomb will spawn in front of the cage, leaving the Crown Tower just outside its radius while the cage itself takes the damage for the Evolved Goblin Brawler, allowing the Brawler to leave at full health for a counterpush.
  - If the cage is destroyed while a troop is still inside, the troop will exit the cage with its remaining hitpoints and become targetable again.
  - Under most circumstances, theEarthquakeis a mostly ineffective counter to the Evolved Goblin Cage. Only the cage will be destroyed, leaving the Evolved Goblin Brawler to assist in future pushes while you’re low on Elixir.Deckswith a fast cycle, such asHog Riderdecks, may still see the Earthquake as valuable since their cheap cards are able to dispose enemy pushes for large positive Elixir trades.
  - When anEvolved Knightis pulled inside the cage, the shield will still be active. This can be used to tank for higher pressure cards, such as aRam Rider, Prince, or anEvolved Battle Ram, possibly preventing the Evolved Goblin Cage from getting too much defensive value.
  - Despite the Evolved Goblin Brawler’s higher hitpoints, theSkeletons,Goblins,Guards,Archers, orEvolved Ice Spiritdeployed in the center of the arena with the help of the Crown Tower is enough to fully counter him, resulting in a large +3 Elixir trade.
  - The Evolved Goblin Brawler’s higher hitpoints allows it to take one additional attack from aPrince,Mini P.E.K.K.A,P.E.K.K.A, orElite Barbarianswith no Crown Tower inference compared to the regular Goblin Brawler.
  - The Evolved Goblin Cage is an amazing counter to theElectro Giant. The Electro Giant will be quickly pulled into the cage and if ignored, will be fully destroyed for a +3 Elixir trade before the Cage’s lifetime runs out, leaving the Goblin Brawler at full hitpoints for a counterattack. Interestingly, the reflection damage will not affect the cage itself nor the Goblin Brawler despite the Electro Giant taking damage.
  - Unlike the regular Goblin Cage, the Evolved Goblin Cage can be effective at destroying troops when deployed at the bridge. Any fragile ranged troops, likeDart Goblin,Firecracker,Bomber,Magic Archer,Mother Witch, orPrincessplaced too close to the river can be pulled in and easily killed. Troops likeKnight,Ice Golem,Golden Knight, Giant,Elixir Golem,Battle Healer, Golem,Valkyrie, or Hog Rider used to tank the Crown Tower for certain win conditions, such asMiner,Goblin Barrel, orGraveyard, will no longer be targetable after being pulled in, allowing the Crown Tower to remain helpful.
  - When defending aMega Knightwith the Evolved Goblin Cage, it’s recommended to not kite it very far as the Mega Knight won’t be pulled into the cage while jumping. Instead, deploy the card 3 tiles to the side of the Mega Knight. This will allow the Goblin Brawler to drag him into the cage without the Mega Knight jumping and will fully counter him if unchecked.
  - Unlike the regular Goblin Cage, it is not a good idea to waste it on kamikaze troops like theWall Breakers, as it will remove any chances of utilizing the pull mechanic, and the Goblin Brawler isn’t much stronger than a regular one to be worth releasing prematurely.
  - The 3 tile pull range of the Evolved Goblin Cage is longer than all cards with a charge, allowing them to be pulled into the cage while they’re charging without damaging it.This is especially effective with the Ram Rider,Dark Prince,Battle Ram, and Prince as these cards will be fully countered for a +1 Elixir trade and fail to break the cage, leaving the Goblin Brawler at full hitpoints.
  - Done to theGiant Skeleton, the death bomb will spawn in front of the cage, leaving the Crown Tower just outside its radius while the cage itself takes the damage for the Evolved Goblin Brawler, allowing the Brawler to leave at full health for a counterpush.
  - Deckswith a fast cycle, such asHog Riderdecks, may still see the Earthquake as valuable since their cheap cards are able to dispose enemy pushes for large positive Elixir trades.
  - This is especially effective with the Ram Rider,Dark Prince,Battle Ram, and Prince as these cards will be fully countered for a +1 Elixir trade and fail to break the cage, leaving the Goblin Brawler at full hitpoints.

## History[]

2024
  - The Goblin Cage Evolutioncardwas added to the game on 5/8/2024, in theAugust 2024 Update.
  - On 2/10/2024, theLucky Drops 2.0 Update, fixed a few bugs with the Goblin Cage's Evolution.
  - On 8/10/2024, aBalance Update, decreased the Evolved Goblin Cage’s attack time interval to 1 second (from 1.1 seconds).
  - On 16/12/2024, the2024 Quarter 4 Update, added a sound effect to the Evolved Goblin Brawler after death.
2025
  - On 31/3/2025, the2025 Quarter 1 Update, decreased the Evolved Goblin Brawler's hitpoints by 0.16%. It also made it so thatRune Giantwould no longer be able to enchant troops if captured by Evolved Goblin Cage.
  - On 6/10/2025, aBalance Updateremoved the Evolved Goblin Brawler's hitpoints boost.
