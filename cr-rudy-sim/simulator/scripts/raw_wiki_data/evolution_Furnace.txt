## Release Date

The Furnace's Evolution is aRarecardthat is unlocked with 6 Evolution Shards. It spawns a Furnace, with identical stats to the original. When the Furnace attacks, the spawn speed is decreased to 2.4sec, as well as spawnFire Spiritsto the side, instead of the front. It appears to be an amethyst cast-iron steam locomotive armor with golden spikes, limbs, and aCard Evolutionsymbol in the center, wearing purple gloves ane cape with golden linings, team-colored chainmail, a cracked metal witch-like hat on top with a golden band in the middle, and a golden grate in the center. It also holds a wooden staff with a cauldron placed on top that has a golden handle filled with bubbling magenta fluid in it, which resembles Elixir, along with a large amethyst knob on the bottom with golden spikes on it. The Furnace's Evolution costs 4Elixirand requires 2 cycles to deploy.

## Strategy[]

  - Make sure that the Furnace attacks as much as possible, so that it spawns as many as possible.
  - The Fire Spirit from "Hot Spawns" ability spawn first to the left, then to the right, then to the left again, and so on.This can be used to lure units from the other side of thearena.
  - TheRageworks really well, to spawn even more Fire Spirits.
  - This can be used to lure units from the other side of thearena.
