## Release Date

The Dart Goblin Evolution is aRarecardthat is unlocked with 6 Evolution shards. It spawns a Dart Goblin with identical stats to the original. When attacking a target, he will leave an area of effect poison, becoming stronger after multiple attacks. He resembles aGoblin, but wears a voodoo mask with purple eyes, team-colored face paint and a rope behind, leaves with circles and purple tips, and aCard Evolutionsymbol on top, purple pants, team-colored bands on his arm, and brown shoes, carries a purple potion, and holds a blowgun with purple paint. The Dart Goblin's Evolution costs 3Elixirand requires 2 cycles to deploy.

## Strategy[]

  - Even if the Evolved Dart Goblin is defeated, the poison will stay until that unit is defeated. So shooting high-hitpoint units like theGolem,Giant,P.E.K.K.A,Giant Skeleton,Electro Giant,Mega Knight,Goblin Giant,Royal Giant, orLava Houndwill make sure that a lot of poison will be made for a long time.
  - Against Crown Towers, the poison will only remain for 1 second, and he will only deal 25% of its damage.
  - Troops that spawn other troops after death will not carry over the poison to their sub troops if the parent troop is poisoned.
  - The poison effect does not stack. For example, if two Evolved Dart Goblins attack the same troop, the second one will not increase the poison damage of the first one. Instead, both poison effects will affect the troop individually.
