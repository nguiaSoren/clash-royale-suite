The Musketeer is aRarecardthat is unlocked in theTraining Camp. It spawns one single-target, air-targeting, long-ranged, and groundtroopwith moderate hitpoints and moderate damage. The Heroic Musketeer takes the appearance of a woman who has golden glowing eyes, coiffed curly hair and eyebrows, and wears a gold breastplate, chainmail, a team-colored cape, a beige helmet with a gold handle in a similar style helmet as one of theThree Musketeershelmets, a belt, beige shoulder pads with gold handles as their base, brown gloves and boots with golden soles and chains, while carrying a beige blunderbuss-styled musket with a golden trigger and muzzle as her weapon with dark gray spheres as her ammunition. She also wields a turret with a large gold handle and base supported by a metal handle in-between and a long, gold and black striped tube, while having the same hat as the Musketeer. A Heroic Musketeer card costs 4to deploy and her ability costs an additional 3to activate.

## Ability: Trusty Turret[]

The Hero Musketeer has a special ability accessible from whatever side of the screen was put in, just above the player'scardslots inbattle. After a 1-second delay, the Hero Musketeer activates her “Trusty Turret”, placing a turret 3 tiles forward of the Musketeer, which is used either as a distraction for regular troops, and as a building to attract win conditions. This turret has a lifetime of 10 seconds, meaning that this is meant to be used reactively as it's not meant to last that long, unless the building is destroyed before the Decay kills it. The ability goes on a 22 second cooldown after the duration ends. The Trusty Turret ability costs 3to activate.

## Strategy[]

  - For the purposes of determiningElixirtrades, the Heroic Musketeer and her activated Ability count as 7 Elixir, as the player must play the 4 Elixir Heroic Musketeer and then spend a further 3 on her Ability.
  - Do not use the ability against an enemyElectro Giant. Not only does it worsen the Elixir cost against him, but it will also lure him towards the Turret, which will cause the Heroic Musketeer to take damage and eventually destroy herself through his Zap Pack. It is better to use her at a range, as either she will defeat him without taking damage, or will force the opponent to pull her within his Zap Pack range by using aTornadoor aFisherman.
