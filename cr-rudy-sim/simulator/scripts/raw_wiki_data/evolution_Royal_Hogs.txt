## Release Date

The Royal Hogs' Evolution is aRarecardthat is unlocked with 6 Evolution Shards. It spawns four Royal Hogs with stats identical to their originals. They deploy as flying troops rather than on the ground, unable to be attacked by ground-targeting troops. Upon attacking, or getting hurt, the hogs will fall to the ground, dealing low area damage on impact. They appear to be smaller versions of theHog Rider's hog wearing team-colored knight-like helmets with theCard Evolutionsymbol on top and have wings. The Royal Hogs' Evolution costs 5Elixirand requires 2 cycles to deploy.

## Strategy[]

  - Play them with a high health tank so that the tank will take most of the damage, while the Royal Hogs fly towards the tower.
  - Try to have a small spell ready for swarms such asSkeleton Army,Goblin Gang,Minion Horde, etc.
  - Good to pair with theWizardorIce Wizard, as they have large spread, moderate damage per second, the Ice Wizard has slowing effect, and they both have reasonably high health. Try to bait one of your opponent's spells first, as if the Hogs get hit by a spell, they will fall and be targeted by ground troops again. Do the same for building, as these cancel out the flying before they reach thePrincess Toweras well.
  - Watch out for opponents' splash troops, such asFirecracker, Wizard,Bomber,Bowler,Executioner, etc, as these can easily wipe out the hogs. Pair them with another fast, high dps troop, such asPrince,Dark Prince,Mini P.E.K.K.A., andLumberjack.
