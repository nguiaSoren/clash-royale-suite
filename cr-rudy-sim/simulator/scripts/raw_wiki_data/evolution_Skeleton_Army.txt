## Release Date

The Skeleton Army's Evolution is anEpicCard that is unlocked with 6 Evolution Shards.. It spawns 15Skeletonsin a scatter formation, with General Gerry at the back. When the Skeletons are defeated, their shadows will spawn. The shadows have unlimited hitpoints, and can't be targeted by troops, buildings and tower troops, but can be affected by spells. When General Gerry is defeated, all the shadows are defeated as well. Gerry appears as aGuardwith purple eyes, wears a helmet with a golden handle and a team-colored crest on top and a purple cape with gold linings, and carries a spear with an attached purple banner that has a skull printed on it and a wooden shield with theCard Evolutionsymbol on it. The Skeleton Army's Evolution costs 3Elixirand requires 2 cycles to deploy.

## Strategy[]

  - In order to fully destroy the Evolved Skeleton Army, General Gerry must be defeated first, otherwise they will stay alive and eliminate anything that is attacking them.
  - Destroying General Gerry will only instantly defeat the shadows and not affect anySkeletonsthat are still alive. However, any alive Skeletons will be prevented from being converted into shadows after death.
  - General Gerry is stronger than a normal Skeleton. He is able to attack slightly faster and from a longer range compared to a regular Skeleton. Keep this in mind when using him on defense against building-targeting troops.
  - LikeGuards, General Gerry has a shield which makes certain spells ineffective against him, however, some spells are still great against him.For a neutral Elixir trade, Arrows will destroy General Gerry's shield and the second wave will defeat him outright. Its large radius also allows it to hit most, if not all of the Evolved Skeleton Army, allowing allied units to break through without being distracted.Although slower than Arrows,Poison,Earthquake, andVinescan defeat General Gerry similarly. It requires 3 hits for either, which means that all 6 of its hits must hit him in order to defeat General Gerry. However, it is able to defeat him at Stage 1 or 2, meaning that he should be isolated first before the Void is used on him.If timed right, aBarbarian Barrelwill instantly destroy the shield, and a swing of theBarbarianwill finish off the unshielded General Gerry. Make sure that the shadows do not walk back to him, otherwise the Barbarian will be overwhelmed.UnlikeZap, theEvolved Zapattacks twice, which means that its two pulses will defeat General Gerry and the remaining Evolved Skeleton Army.   - For a neutral Elixir trade, Arrows will destroy General Gerry's shield and the second wave will defeat him outright. Its large radius also allows it to hit most, if not all of the Evolved Skeleton Army, allowing allied units to break through without being distracted.
  - Although slower than Arrows,Poison,Earthquake, andVinescan defeat General Gerry similarly. However, the former is a negative Elixir trade.
  - Despite being a positive Elixir trade, theGoblin Curseis not able to defeat the General Gerry or his shield in one hit. It requires 3 hits for either, which means that all 6 of its hits must hit him in order to defeat General Gerry. Its smaller radius also means that it may not hit all of the Evolved Skeleton Army and the shadows cannot be cursed, so keep this in mind when using the spell against it.
  - A Stage 3Voidis unable to destroy General Gerry, as it does not deal enough damage to destroy the shield in one hit. However, it is able to defeat him at Stage 1 or 2, meaning that he should be isolated first before the Void is used on him.
  - If timed right, aBarbarian Barrelwill instantly destroy the shield, and a swing of theBarbarianwill finish off the unshielded General Gerry. Make sure that the shadows do not walk back to him, otherwise the Barbarian will be overwhelmed.
  - UnlikeZap, theEvolved Zapattacks twice, which means that its two pulses will defeat General Gerry and the remaining Evolved Skeleton Army. Since bothcardshave the same cycle cost, make sure that you save it for when the opponent is evolving their Skeleton Army.

## Trivia[]

  - The Evolved Skeleton Army may be based on theImmortals, a legion in the Achaemenid Persian Army where their numbers would not decrease with its defeat, due to the casualties being replaced. It could also be inspired by theDead Man of Dunharrowfrom The Lord Of The Rings, a ghost skeleton army that could attack but not be hit themselves, with the general representing theKing of the Dead.
  - The Skeleton Army Evolution summons 15 Skeletons alongside General Gerry (16 skeletons in total), but as revealed in the1/10/2018 balance changes, one of the Skeletons in the regular Skeleton Army card is already named Gerry, which means that either another Skeleton called Gerry joined the army and became the general or the existing Gerry was promoted and an extra unnamed Skeleton replaced him.
  - Before the Skeleton Army's Evolution's release, the Skeletons' attack time interval was increased to 1.1 seconds (from 1 second).
