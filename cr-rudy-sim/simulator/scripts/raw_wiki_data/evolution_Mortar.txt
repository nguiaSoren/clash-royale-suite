The Mortar's Evolution is aCommoncardthat is unlocked with 6 Evolution Shards. It spawns a Mortar with a hit speed 1 second faster than the original. Every attack that it does will also spawn aGoblin. It is a metallic cylindrical tube with a golden base, a wooden gimbal on each side with three purple studs on them which are held by purple and black metal stakes, gold chains and aCard Evolutionsymbol on the side, and a large, double-layered team-colored band near the muzzle, which is surrounded by two gold rings, with a Goblin located near the top. The Mortar's Evolution costs 4Elixirand requires 2 cycles to deploy.

## Strategy[]

  - As it requires 2 cycles to evolve, It takes a long time to get this card into theArena. However, it makes up for the wait with incredible power. Due to its incredibly high damage, this card can take aCrown Towerdown on its own if ignored.
  - The Evolved Mortar has great synergy with theFisherman. The Fisherman can distract and pull single-targeting units such as aMini P.E.K.K.A, Knight, Mighty Miner,Goblin Machine, Cannon Cart,P.E.K.K.A,Giant Skeleton,Golden Knight, Monk,Battle Healer,Lumberjack, Boss Bandit, Miner, or aPrinceinto the Mortar's blind spot, causing the Evolved Mortar to connect to the Crown Tower and get massive damage.
  - In certain situations,Earthquakecan be a reliable and effective counter to the Evolved Mortar due to its high damage to buildings. This can be paired with a properly placedFire SpiritorIce Spiritto mostly counter it for an equal Elixir trade.
  - Whenever the Evolved Mortar attacks theTower Princess, the Goblin it spawns will always get 2 hits, making even 1 connection deal hundreds of damage, factoring in both the Evolved Mortar's boulders and its spawned Goblins.If theCannoneerorDagger Duchessare in use, the Evolved Mortar’s Goblin will only get 1 hit, significantly reducing the damage dealt over time compared to the Tower Princess.
  - Compared to the regular Mortar, the Evolved Mortar is slightly weaker to an activatedKing Tower, as the spawned Goblins will take damage from both the King Tower and the Crown Tower. Because of this, it’s highly recommended to get the Evolved Mortar or another unit to activate your King Tower as this will reduce future damage from 2 Goblin hits on the Tower Princess to 1. Interactions with the Cannoneer and Dagger Duchess don’t change as the Goblin will get 1 hit regardless.
  - The Evolved Mortar has decent synergy with theGoblin Drillas the Evolved Mortar can assist with taking out swarms and high health units, such asGoblin Gang,Skeleton Army, Prince, Lumberjack,Skeleton King, Mini P.E.K.K.A, Golden Knight,Goblin Machine,Skeleton King, Cannon Cart,Mega Knight,Valkyrie,Elite Barbarians,Monk,Sparky,Mighty Miner, P.E.K.K.A,Boss Bandit, Battle Healer,Royal Ghost, Giant Skeleton, Miner, andKnight, meant to defend the Goblin Drill while the Goblin Drill does the majority of the damage. The additional Goblins spawned by the Evolved Mortar will further help in dealing damage to the Crown Tower, forcing the opponent to respond with a spell.Contrary, a Goblin Drill played on defense is a decent counter to the Evolved Mortar as the Goblins that come out from the Goblin Drill will attack the Evolved Mortar as it is attacking the drill itself. This is an equal Elixir trade while heavily reducing the damage done to your Crown Tower.
  - Other building cards, such asTeslaandBomb Tower, are reliable counters to the Evolved Mortar as their somewhat high health and good damage allow them to fully stop the Evolved Mortar. These cards also are equal Elixir trades.
  - Low health ranged cards, such asDart Goblin,Princess, andSpear Goblins, should not be used alone to counter the Evolved Mortar as its damage will OHKO them. Instead, it’s encouraged to use a mini tank, such as anIce Golem, Knight, Battle Healer,Elixir Golem, Valkyrie, Skeleton King,Hog Rider, orMiner, to tank the Evolved Mortar’s shots, giving the chance for these cards to take out the Evolved Mortar unharmed.Similarly they should not be placed next to the Evolved Mortar's target as the Evolved Mortar does splash damage, and will hit both. Even ranged troops that can survive its attack are unrecommended because the spawned Goblins may defeat them after getting weakened by the Evolved Mortar's boulders.
  - On the other hand, bulky ranged cards, such asEvolved Royal Giant,Cannon Cart,Executioner, andBowler, are great counters to the Evolved Mortar as they’re capable of staying out of its blind radius, tank the Evolved Mortar’s shots, and deal high damage to take it out before it can lock onto the Crown Tower.
  - If theCannoneerorDagger Duchessare in use, the Evolved Mortar’s Goblin will only get 1 hit, significantly reducing the damage dealt over time compared to the Tower Princess.
  - Contrary, a Goblin Drill played on defense is a decent counter to the Evolved Mortar as the Goblins that come out from the Goblin Drill will attack the Evolved Mortar as it is attacking the drill itself. This is an equal Elixir trade while heavily reducing the damage done to your Crown Tower.
  - Similarly they should not be placed next to the Evolved Mortar's target as the Evolved Mortar does splash damage, and will hit both. Even ranged troops that can survive its attack are unrecommended because the spawned Goblins may defeat them after getting weakened by the Evolved Mortar's boulders.

## Trivia[]

  - The first use ofmortars in warfarewas in 1413, where the Koreans used thewan'gu(gourd-shaped mortar) during a naval battle. Mortars are still used in modern warfare to this day as indirect fire weapons for close fire support.
  - The Mortar's Evolution might be based off of an April Fools joke, where on 1/4/2016, certain Content Creators were given a different version of the game, with a Mortar that attacked by shootingGoblin Barrelsinstead of boulders.
  - The Mortar is 1 of 6 evolvedcardsassociated withGoblins, with the others beingDart Goblin,Goblin Cage,Goblin Giant,Goblin Barrel, andGoblin Drill.
  - The Mortar was the firstbuildingto receive aCard Evolution.
  - In the Korean and German language settings of the game, the Mortar's name translates to "Mine Thrower".
  - Due to a glitch, theEvolved Teslaplays the Evolved Mortar's deployment sound when played by the opponent.
  - The Mortar is unavailable in multiplespecial game modes. More specifically:It is 1 of 20 cards that is unavailable inMirrormode, the others beingMinions,Royal Recruits,Cannon,Tesla,Goblin Hut,Tombstone,Bomb Tower,Inferno Tower,Barbarian Hut,Furnace,Elixir Collector,Freeze,Clone,X-Bow,Golden Knight,Skeleton King,Archer Queen,Mighty Miner, and theMonk.It is unavailable inThe Floor is Healingsub-modeof the Global Spell game mode, much like other building cards, as well as theCannon Cart.
  - The Mortar's Evolution is the subject of anEmote.
  - On 3/1/2024, the Evolved Mortar was supposed to receive a Goblin first attack time interval increase to 0.5 seconds (from 0.4 seconds), but this changewas pulled back last minute.
  - It is 1 of 20 cards that is unavailable inMirrormode, the others beingMinions,Royal Recruits,Cannon,Tesla,Goblin Hut,Tombstone,Bomb Tower,Inferno Tower,Barbarian Hut,Furnace,Elixir Collector,Freeze,Clone,X-Bow,Golden Knight,Skeleton King,Archer Queen,Mighty Miner, and theMonk.
