## Arena

The Cannon's Evolution is aCommoncardthat is unlocked with 6 Evolution Shards. It spawns a Cannon with identical stats to the original. After deployment, the Cannon shoots 9 cannonballs in 2 rows, 5 on the top row and 4 on the bottom, which deals area damage and inflicts knockback. It appears as a large dark metal barrel mounted on a four-legged wooden mount with a purple metal plate, legs and supports reinforced with gold chains, with a purple muzzle, two team-colored ridges, one midway and the other at the rear, along with aCard Evolutionsymbol in between, and a metal wing attached to both sides. The Cannon's Evolution costs 3Elixirand requires 2 cycles to deploy.

## Strategy[]

  - The Evolved Cannon can be used offensively to snipe buildings and troops on the opponents side of theArena.
  - The knock back can push units towards the Cannon, making it great on defense.
  - When a tower is down, placing the Cannon on the opponent's side would actually hit the other towers (however, it does reduced damage to them, but this is still beneficial, especially during Sudden Death).
  - Against theEvolved Goblin Barrel, the Evolved Cannon can prevent all but one hit on eitherCrown Towerwhen using theTower Princess. However, it has to be placed in front of theKing Towerto do this, which will prevent it from distracting any other incoming troop, either from the Evolved Goblin Barrel push or from follow-up attacks.

## Trivia[]

  - The word "Cannon" comes from the Akkadian word "𒄀" (qanû), meaning reed. Cannons were invented in China during the twelfth century. It was most likely developed in parallel or as an evolution of an earlier gunpowder weapon called the fire lance. Cannons were used for warfare by the late thirteenth century in the Yuan dynasty and spread throughout Eurasia in the fourteenth century. During the late Middle Ages, large and small cannons were developed for siege and field battles. The cannon replaced prior siege weapons such as the trebuchet. After the late Middle Ages, most large cannons were abandoned in favor of greater numbers of lighter, more maneuverable field artillery.
  - The Evolved Cannon's ability is inspired by the Eagle Artillery from Clash of Clans, similarly to its wings.
  - Among all evolvedbuildingsthat can attack, the Evolved Cannon is tied withTeslafor the shortest range of all buildings, with a range of 5.5 tiles.
  - Although Cannons themselves can only target groundtroops, theKing Tower's cannon can target both air and ground troops. This might be because the cannon is elevated on the tower.
