The Hunter Evolution is anEpiccardthat is unlocked with 6 Evolution Shards. It spawns a Hunter with identical stats to the original. The Evolved Hunter will throw a net at the closest unit every 5 seconds. The net will freeze the troop in place and render it unable to move or attack, and allow ground units to hit the trapped unit for 3 seconds. He appears as a man who wears a team-colored coonskin cap with purple hair, a golden stripe and aCard Evolutionsymbol in the middle, a brown cloth, gloves, belt, pants and boots, an indigo shirt, and team-colored wristlets, is armed with a double-barreled shotgun, wield nets with purple points, and has fuzzy black eyebrows and mustache covering his eyes and mouth respectively. The Hunter's Evolution costs 4Elixirand requires 2 cycles to deploy.

## Strategy[]

  - Combining him withEvolved Skeletons,Skeleton Army,Bats,Goblin Gang, orMinion Hordecan fully counter a LavaLoon combo, commonly known as theLava HoundandBallooncombo push, for a massive positive Elixir trade.
  - If there is only 1 unit against Hunter, then that unit will be stun-locked forever, as the duration and hit speed of the net is the same as the cooldown.
  - The Evolved Hunter performs slightly worse againstGraveyardthan hisregular counterpartdue to his net animation. The net itself won't make a difference against the Skeletons, but the net animation makes it so that his attack animation is slightly delayed, allowing for some more Skeleton hits on yourCrown Tower.

## History[]

2025
  - The Hunter's Evolution was available on 24/2/2025, before its release date, as an unintended glitch, in theEvo LumberjackDraft Event. Supercell fixed this glitch with a maintenance break.
  - The Hunter's Evolutioncardwas added to the game on 3/3/2025, in theMarch 2025 Update.
  - On 31/3/2025, the2025 Quarter 1 Update, increased the Evolved Hunter's hitpoints by 0.11%. It also fixed a few bugs where the Evolved Hunter would not net othertroopsor air troops not getting grounded by Evolved Hunter’s net.
  - On 3/6/2025, aBalance Update, decreased the Evolved Hunter's first attack time interval to 0.7 seconds (from 0.8 seconds), decreased the Evolved Hunter's net duration to 3 seconds (from 4 seconds), and increased its cooldown to 5 seconds (from 4 seconds).
  - On 28/11/25, the Hunter Evo was given out for free on Black Friday.
