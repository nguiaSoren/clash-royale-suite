## Release Date

The Hero Giant is aRarecardthat is unlocked with 200 hero fragments. It spawns one single-target, ground-targeting, melee, and groundtroopwith high hitpoints and moderate damage. The Heroic Giant takes the appearance of a colossal humanoid creature with glowing yellow eyes, blonde mutton chops, hair and eyebrows, wearing darker brown pants, a golden headband, and a team-colored belt with a golden base and details along with theHeroShard in the center, while being bare-chested and wielding a pair of brass knuckles as his weapon with pearl and gold armbands. A Heroic Giant card costs 5to deploy, and his ability costs an additional 2to activate.

## Ability: Heroic Hurl[]

The Hero Giant has a special ability accessible from whatever side of the screen was put in, just above the player'scardslots inbattle. After a 1-second delay and when a unit gets in range, the Hero Giant activates his “Heroic Hurl”, grabbing the highest HP enemytroopwithin 2 tiles around him and throws them horizontally, also dealing damage to them when they land. While on the air, these troops are untargetable by ground-targeting troops and theEarthquake, but can still take damage from other spells. Air troops can be affected by the ability, but will take no damage when they land. The ability goes on a 14 second cooldown after the duration ends. The Heroic Hurl ability costs 2Elixirto activate.

## Strategy[]

  - For the purposes of determiningElixirtrades, the Heroic Giant and his activated Ability count as 7 Elixir, as the player must play the 5 Elixir Heroic Giant and then spend a further 2 on his Ability.
  - This card would be good in Giant Graveyard decks as with its ability, it could prevent a knockback unit like theEvolved Mega Knightand theMonkfrom knocking it out of range of the Princess tower, so then the skeletons from theGraveyardcan take the tower.
  - It would also be good on Giant Double Prince decks as the Giant can stil act as a tank, but now, it is able to add DPS to a troop with extra from the two brothers, so that if the unit is strong and a major threat, like theEvolved Mega KnightorMonk, you can use the Ability to knock it onto the other lane, which makes sure it can't hinder your troops' progress towards a 3 crown.
  - The Heroic Giant is completely ineffective against swarms, as he has no way to attack them directly, besides using his ability, which will only launch one unit.
  - He is also easily countered by any building, as the ability will do nothing against them. Even against theBarbarian Hut, it will, at worst, send oneBarbarianaway, while the remaining ones take him down.
  - When using theFishermanagainst the Heroic Giant, he should be placed closer to the edge of the Arena instead of the center, This will cause the Heroic Giant to throw him to the other lane, but still be in range to pull the Heroic Giant to the other lane.
  - When using the Heroic Giant, it is always important to attack the same lane as the opponent. This allows the Heroic Giant to send their offensive card into the other lane, leaving it unsupported and easy to defeat , while their support troops are defeated by the Heroic Giant's support troops.
