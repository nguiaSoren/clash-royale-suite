## Release Date

The Goblin Giant's Evolution is aEpiccardthat is unlocked with 6 Evolution Shards. It spawns a Goblin Giant with identical stats to the original. When his hitpoints are lowered below 50%, every 2.2 seconds, the Goblin Giant will passively summon aGoblin. The Goblin Giant resembles a colossal, aquamarine-skinned Goblin with a deep pink nose, purple eyes, sideburns and goatee, wearing a purple aviator hat with aCard Evolutionsymbol and pants with gold stitches and golden linings on both of them, along with a purple backpack with ropes tied on the sides and team-colored suspenders, carries a dagger with a purple handle, and has two ropes tied on his arms and white bands tied on his legs. The Goblin Giant's Evolution costs 6Elixirand requires 1 cycle to deploy.

## Strategy[]

  - The Evolved Goblin Giant is great on offense from his ability to quickly stack up massive amounts of Goblins at 50% hitpoints while its high hitpoints and Spear Goblins tank for support troops and cripple opposing defenses.
  - The Evolved Goblin Giant should never be ignored as he will easily destroy theCrown Tower, regardless oftower troop, and even destroy theKing Towerwhen left alone.
  - TheInferno Toweris by far the best building counter to an unsupported Evolved Goblin Giant as the ramp-up damage can easily take him down while its high hitpoints by time for the Crown Towers to destroy the Goblins that he spawns, all for a +1 Elixir trade.Be weary if the opponent has any stun in theirdeckfrom cards likeZap,Electro Spirit,Electro Dragon, Lightning,Zappies,Goblinstein, andElectro Wizard. These cards will reset the ramp-up, allowing the Evolved Goblin Giant to destroy the Inferno Tower before he goes down.
  - Cardswith high single or multi target damage, such asEvolved Valkyrie,Night Witch, Mega Minion,Mighty Miner, Monk,Barbarians,Evolved Hunter,Three Musketeers, Prince,Evolved Mega Knight,Elite Barbarians, Phoenix,Bowler,Evolved Wizard,Sparky,Evolved Executioner, Inferno Dragon,Minion Horde,Skeleton Army, andP.E.K.K.A, are the best troop counters to the Evolved Goblin Giant.The P.E.K.K.A can easily destroy the Evolved Goblin Giant with her very high damage per hit, but can get quickly overwhelmed by the Goblins that are leftover after the Evolved Goblin Giant goes down, likely resulting in her taking some moderate damage for a counterattack.Barbarians can quickly swarm and destroy the Evolved Goblin Giant and the spawn Goblins with ease for a +1 positive Elixir trade, but are very susceptible to high damage dealing spells that may support the Evolved Goblin Giant, likeFireballorPoison; they’re a very effective but unreliable counter in some circumstances.The Evolved Valkyrie can easily dispose of the spawned Goblins with her area damage being able to OHKO the Goblins while doing damage to the Evolved Goblin Giant. Additionally, her tornado effect can easily pull the Goblin Giant towards her, preventing him for reaching the Crown Tower despite her moderate DPS, resulting in a slightly damaged Valkyrie for a +2 positive Elixir trade.
  - Unlike the regularGoblin Giant, the Mini P.E.K.K.A is not especially recommended when dealing with an Evolved Goblin Giant as he will be quickly swarmed by the numerous spawned Goblins and the 2 Spear Goblins after he destroys the Evolved Goblin Giant. This will result in a dead Mini P.E.K.K.A and will deal slight damage to aTower PrincessorDagger Duchessand moderate damage to aCannoneer.
  - Certainspellswork particularly well with the Evolved Goblin Giant because of the Spear Goblins in his backpack and ability to rapidly spawn Goblins.The Zap andGiant Snowballmake great synergies with the Evolved Goblin Giant. Their cheaper cost and damage threshold is significant, as using them on defendingMinionsor Goblins⁣ allows the Spear Goblins to OHKO the swarms efficiently. They’ll also briefly slow down the enemy’s defense, buying more time for Goblins to spawn and overwhelm the opponent.Ragedoes similar damage to the Zap, allowing the Spear Goblins to OHKO the same swarms. The Rage will be able to affect the Goblin Giant, the Goblins' spawn time and his Spear Goblins, allowing the spell to benefit multiple facets of the Evolved Goblin Giant without the risk that comes from using it on frail swarms.Similarly, the Fireball synergizes well with the Evolved Goblin Giant due to the Spear Goblins being able to finish off defenders likeMusketeer, Electro Wizard,Wizard, orEvolved Barbarians, allowing the Goblin Giant and the spawned Goblins to deal significantly more damage and apply much more pressure onto the opponent.Due to the Evolved Goblin Giant's weakness to buildings, he pairs quite well with theEarthquake. The Earthquake will destroy buildings for the Goblin Giant to continue pursuing towards the Crown Tower. The Spear Goblins and spawned Goblins can also help finish off troops that can survive a lone Earthquake, such as aDart Goblin,Firecracker,Archers,Bomber, orPrincess.Lightningcan be paired with the Evolved Goblin Giant for the sole purpose of destroying high-damage mini tanks and buildings used against him like anInferno Dragon,Barbarian Hut,Bomb Tower, Mighty Miner, Inferno Tower,Mini P.E.K.K.A., Phoenix, Sparky,Tesla, orLumberjack.Freezecan be a situational synergy with the Evolved Goblin Giant as its 4 second freeze duration allow the Spear Goblins to attack more of the opposing defenses while also giving extra time for the Goblins to spawn. Single target attackers, such asPrince,Phoenix, Mighty Miner, Inferno Dragon, P.E.K.K.A,Monk,Mega Minion, and Lumberjack will be overwhelmed by the influx of Goblins if not responded to properly.
  - The Evolved Goblin Giant is a reliable counter toGraveyard. The 2 Spear Goblins will rapidly take out many of theSkeletonsthat spawn and with the help of the Dagger Duchess or Tower Princess, it can be mostly countered while applying intense pressure on the opponent with a healthy counterattacking Goblin Giant. However, be on the lookout for anything that’s distracting the Crown Tower; the Goblin Giant by itself can’t efficiently deal with a Graveyard without its help.
  - An expensive but extremely potent offensive combination is theP.E.K.K.A.paired with the Evolved Goblin Giant. The Evolved Goblin Giant can quickly clear small swarms like Bats and Skeletons, while the P.E.K.K.A. has excellent one-on-one matchups with many single-target damage dealers used to counter the Evolved Goblin Giant like the Prince,Mighty Miner, and Mini P.E.K.K.A.Spell support is crucial to wipe out larger swarms like the Skeleton Army,Goblin Gang, andMinion Horde, that could deal hefty damage to the push.Rageis especially potent as it helps make the push even more threatening, and makes the Spear Goblins on the Evolved Goblin Giant attack faster.Elite Barbariansare a cheaper alternative to the P.E.K.K.A. While they are less bulkier and are more susceptible to area damage, they are cheaper and move significantly faster, making them harder to distract. Additionally, they can clear small swarms faster as a pair.
  - TheFishermanpairs well with the Evolved Goblin Giant to pull away single-target damage dealers used to counter the Goblin Giant like theMini P.E.K.K.A., Mighty Miner, and Prince.
  - Be weary if the opponent has any stun in theirdeckfrom cards likeZap,Electro Spirit,Electro Dragon, Lightning,Zappies,Goblinstein, andElectro Wizard. These cards will reset the ramp-up, allowing the Evolved Goblin Giant to destroy the Inferno Tower before he goes down.
  - The P.E.K.K.A can easily destroy the Evolved Goblin Giant with her very high damage per hit, but can get quickly overwhelmed by the Goblins that are leftover after the Evolved Goblin Giant goes down, likely resulting in her taking some moderate damage for a counterattack.
  - Barbarians can quickly swarm and destroy the Evolved Goblin Giant and the spawn Goblins with ease for a +1 positive Elixir trade, but are very susceptible to high damage dealing spells that may support the Evolved Goblin Giant, likeFireballorPoison; they’re a very effective but unreliable counter in some circumstances.
  - The Evolved Valkyrie can easily dispose of the spawned Goblins with her area damage being able to OHKO the Goblins while doing damage to the Evolved Goblin Giant. Additionally, her tornado effect can easily pull the Goblin Giant towards her, preventing him for reaching the Crown Tower despite her moderate DPS, resulting in a slightly damaged Valkyrie for a +2 positive Elixir trade.
  - The Zap andGiant Snowballmake great synergies with the Evolved Goblin Giant. Their cheaper cost and damage threshold is significant, as using them on defendingMinionsor Goblins⁣ allows the Spear Goblins to OHKO the swarms efficiently. They’ll also briefly slow down the enemy’s defense, buying more time for Goblins to spawn and overwhelm the opponent.
  - Ragedoes similar damage to the Zap, allowing the Spear Goblins to OHKO the same swarms. The Rage will be able to affect the Goblin Giant, the Goblins' spawn time and his Spear Goblins, allowing the spell to benefit multiple facets of the Evolved Goblin Giant without the risk that comes from using it on frail swarms.
  - Similarly, the Fireball synergizes well with the Evolved Goblin Giant due to the Spear Goblins being able to finish off defenders likeMusketeer, Electro Wizard,Wizard, orEvolved Barbarians, allowing the Goblin Giant and the spawned Goblins to deal significantly more damage and apply much more pressure onto the opponent.
  - Due to the Evolved Goblin Giant's weakness to buildings, he pairs quite well with theEarthquake. The Earthquake will destroy buildings for the Goblin Giant to continue pursuing towards the Crown Tower. The Spear Goblins and spawned Goblins can also help finish off troops that can survive a lone Earthquake, such as aDart Goblin,Firecracker,Archers,Bomber, orPrincess.
  - Lightningcan be paired with the Evolved Goblin Giant for the sole purpose of destroying high-damage mini tanks and buildings used against him like anInferno Dragon,Barbarian Hut,Bomb Tower, Mighty Miner, Inferno Tower,Mini P.E.K.K.A., Phoenix, Sparky,Tesla, orLumberjack.
  - Freezecan be a situational synergy with the Evolved Goblin Giant as its 4 second freeze duration allow the Spear Goblins to attack more of the opposing defenses while also giving extra time for the Goblins to spawn. Single target attackers, such asPrince,Phoenix, Mighty Miner, Inferno Dragon, P.E.K.K.A,Monk,Mega Minion, and Lumberjack will be overwhelmed by the influx of Goblins if not responded to properly.
  - Spell support is crucial to wipe out larger swarms like the Skeleton Army,Goblin Gang, andMinion Horde, that could deal hefty damage to the push.Rageis especially potent as it helps make the push even more threatening, and makes the Spear Goblins on the Evolved Goblin Giant attack faster.
  - Elite Barbariansare a cheaper alternative to the P.E.K.K.A. While they are less bulkier and are more susceptible to area damage, they are cheaper and move significantly faster, making them harder to distract. Additionally, they can clear small swarms faster as a pair.

They often have magical abilities similar to a fairy or demon, such as the ability to shapeshift.
