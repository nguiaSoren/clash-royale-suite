The Lumberjack's Evolution is aLegendarycardthat is unlocked with 6 Evolution Shards. It spawns a Lumberjack with identical stats to the original. When the Lumberjack dies, his ghost will spawn. The ghost has unlimited health, and can't be targeted by troops, buildings and towers, but can be affected by spells, and will disappear after a short time outside of the initialRage Spell. The Lumberjack resembles a ginger-hairedBarbarianwith purple eyes and a scarlet nose, wearing a brown belt, purple ushanka with theCard Evolutionsymbol in the center and kilt with gold linings, and team-colored wristbands and sandals, wielding an axe with a purple-tipped blade, a darker handle, and a golden knob on the bottom, and carries a Rage Spell on his shoulder. His ghost appears identical to him, but has pale skin and tongue, white hair, beard, mustache and eyebrows, and his axe's blade is cyan. The Lumberjack's Evolution costs 4Elixirand requires 2 cycles to deploy.

## Strategy[]

  - The Evolved Lumberjack's Ghost lasts for roughly 5.5 seconds, the same duration as his Rage Spell, which is enough time to deal significant damage to targets if not addressed.The ghost's mechanic cannot be extended by extra Rage Spells - he will only keep his invincibility in his own Rage's radius and during its duration.
  - He has a good synergy with win conditions while attacking, as the ghost will take out a lot of squishy troops and deal significant damage to mini-tanks before killing your win condition.
  - The Evolved Lumberjack is extremely versatile on defense: addSkeletons,Goblins,Guards, or anIce Spiritand they can defend many cards, including things such asMega Knight,Giant Skeleton, or even aP.E.K.K.A, assuming if there is a Rage Spell involved.
  - Be careful when pulling an Evolved Lumberjack to your King Tower for an activation, as he will deal significant damage if the Ghost is not pulled after the initial Rage Spell is deployed.
  - Defending the Evolved Lumberjack mainly involves either luring the ghost out of the Rage radius or slowing it down so that the Rage time gets wasted.Examples involve aMini P.E.K.K.AorPrince, whereby first destroying the regular Lumberjack, due to their speed and moderate/high hitpoints, they will kite the Evolved Lumberjack, thus forcing him to leave the radius of the rage, which are good counters.Another method involves theFreeze Spell. By Freezing the Ghost, it will outlast the rage duration, which will nullify the effect of the ghost. This works even better if you have other troops frozen as well and drop swarms for an easy defense.A very good method for counter pushing involves kiting the ghost into the middle, so it is far from a Crown Tower to avoid damage, then playing a fast win-condition such asHog Rider,Dart Goblin,Firecracker, orWall Breakersto kite the ghost away, not only killing the ghost, but also pressuring the opponent.
  - If the ghost of the Evolved Lumberjack ever locks on, log the ghost then play a distraction. While the ghost doesn't take damage, it is still prone to knockback, slowing effects, and stuns. This includesThe Log,Fireball,Rocket, and theEvolved Giant Snowball, the slowdown of the Ice Wizard and theIce Golem's death damage, as well as thePoisonandEarthquake, and theEvolved Firecracker, and the stun effect of theZapandLightning.
  - Interestingly, while it can't be targeted by a troop, it is still classed as a troop, so it can still act as a lightning rod for cards likeElectro Dragon,Mega Minion,Archer Queen,Phoenix,Witch,Night Witch,Wizard,Ice Wizard,Electro Wizard,Fisherman,Goblin Cage,Bandit,Battle Ram,Musketeer, orHunter, which helps with defending many troops, dealing a lot of extra damage.
  - Despite its limited lifetime, it is still a fantastic option at dealing with moderate sized pushes, as the initial Rage adds some bonus Damage which can kill some swarms that would distract the Ghost, allowing it to focus on more important targets like support units.
  - Defending with the Evolved Lumberjack is also possible, but instead of kiting like with the normal one, it is better to play the Evolved Lumberjack Directly on top, so that the ghost can make short work of the troops.
  - The ghost's mechanic cannot be extended by extra Rage Spells - he will only keep his invincibility in his own Rage's radius and during its duration.
  - Examples involve aMini P.E.K.K.AorPrince, whereby first destroying the regular Lumberjack, due to their speed and moderate/high hitpoints, they will kite the Evolved Lumberjack, thus forcing him to leave the radius of the rage, which are good counters.
  - Another method involves theFreeze Spell. By Freezing the Ghost, it will outlast the rage duration, which will nullify the effect of the ghost. This works even better if you have other troops frozen as well and drop swarms for an easy defense.
  - A very good method for counter pushing involves kiting the ghost into the middle, so it is far from a Crown Tower to avoid damage, then playing a fast win-condition such asHog Rider,Dart Goblin,Firecracker, orWall Breakersto kite the ghost away, not only killing the ghost, but also pressuring the opponent.

## History[]

2025
  - The Lumberjack's Evolutioncardwas added to the game on 3/2/2025, in theFebruary 2025 Update.
  - On 4/2/2025, aBalance Updatemade it so that Lumberjack Ghost is immune toEarthquake,Void,Poison,Tornado,Goblin Curse,Evolved Firecracker's sparks, andEvolved Tesla's pulse. It also fixed an issue with an enemy Evolved Lumberjack's sound effects.
  - On 11/2/2025, amaintenance breakmade it so that Lumberjack Ghost's lifetime is no longer affected byRage.
  - On 4/3/2025, aBalance Updatedecreased the Rage's duration after leaving the radius to 1 second (from 2 seconds).
  - On 31/3/2025, the2025 Quarter 1 Updateallowed theExecutionerandRam Riderto be affected by Rage.
  - On 1/4/2025, amaintenance breakfixed a bug whereElectro Wizardcould nullify Lumberjack's Evolution's death effects. It also fixed a bug where Lumberjack Ghost would deal incorrect damage.
  - On 9/4/2025, aBalance Updateincreased the cycles required to 2 (from 1), but also increased the Lumberjack Ghost's damage by 33%. The Rage's damage was also decreased by 23%.
  - On 4/8/2025, theAugust 2025 Updateincreased the Rage's damage by 21%.
  - On 6/10/2025, aBalance Updatedecreased the Rage's speed boost to 30% (from 35%).
