## Release Date

The Goblin Drill Evolution is anEpiccardthat is unlocked with 6 Evolution Shards. It spawns a Goblin Drill with identical stats to the original. Once its hitpoints reach 66% and 33% respectively, the Goblin Drill will resurface nearby, leaving aGoblinbehind each time it's destroyed. If the Goblin Drill is not deployed next to theCrown Towers, it will resurface in the same spot. If the Goblin Drill is deployed next to the Crown Towers, it will resurface 90 degrees around a Crown Tower. It appears as a building with purple spikes that have golden frames as their base, a wooden hull, aCard Evolution-shaped golden hatch, a purple Goblin emblem on the side, a team-colored stripe in the center, and a purple metal drill on its top. The Goblin Drill Evolution costs 4Elixirand requires 2 cycles to deploy.

## Strategy[]

  - The Evolved Goblin Drill is a very powerful offensive tool, being able to reach the Crown Tower and spawn a significant number of Goblins to quickly chip away at it, especially with the assistance of a tank like theKnight,Valkyrie,Monk,Battle Healer,Golden Knight,Prince, orSkeleton King.
  - Unless theTower Princessis in use, the Evolved Goblin Drill should never be ignored. TheCannoneerattacks fast enough to destroy all the spawned Goblins just before he goes down, leaving him on a sliver of hitpoints, while theDagger Duchesswill quickly go into dagger cooldown and get completely destroyed. However, the Tower Princess will steadily destroy each Goblin, leaving her at about half of her original hitpoints.
  - Despite the Evolved Goblin Drill being abuilding,Earthquakeis an ineffective counter. This is due to the spawned Goblins taking little to no damage from the Earthquake as the drill itself will take most of the ticks for them.If the user wants to use the Earthquake despite the weaknesses, using a small spell in tandem, such asThe LogorArrows, is highly recommended to take out many of the Goblins before damage can be done to the Crown Tower.
  - Cards with high area damage, such as theBowler,Bomber,Executioner,Firecracker, andWizard, are great counters to the Evolved Goblin Drill. These cards can OHKO the Goblin Drill itself and all Goblins within the drill at once, causing little damage to be dealt to the Crown Tower with each resurface.The Bowler is the best of these counters as when deployed properly, the lingering attack of the ball will usually destroy both the drill and the Goblins at the same time without any damage being dealt to the Crown Tower. Also, the Bowler will be very healthy afterwards, providing a threatening counterattack.
  - Unlike the regularGoblin Drill, theSkeleton Armyis not a reliable counter to the Evolved Goblin Drill. Each resurface will reinstate the spawn damage, which is enough to OHKO a Skeleton. Over time, the Goblins and repeated spawn damage will destroy enough Skeletons to where a few Goblins will stab the Crown Tower from the lack of defensive troops.
  - Strangely enough, with the assistance of the Cannoneer or Dagger Duchess, the Evolved Goblin Drill deployed right on top of aP.E.K.K.A.can fully counter her for a +3 positive Elixir trade.
  - While it may have high hitpoints, its multiple resurfaces make it an ineffective tank for building-targeting troops like theGiantas it will cause the Crown Towers to retarget onto the building-targeting troop. However, the Evolved Goblin Drill’s high damage can put distress on the opponent to deal with its Goblins before the building-targeting troop. Forcing the opponent to commit all their Elixir to preventing damage from the Evolved Goblin Drill instead of the building-targeting troop.
  - Since it deals knockback after resurfacing all 3 times, it can stop theEvolved Battle Ramand theRam Rider's charge, absorb their damage and defeat either unit with the Crown Tower’s assistance for an even or +1 positive Elixir trade respectively.
  - An interesting, yet highly effective strategy involving the Evolved Goblin Drill is using theTornadoto pull defending troops, such as theValkyrie,Mighty Miner,Prince,Lumberjack, andMini P.E.K.K.A, from the Goblin Drill and assist in clearing defending swarms, likeGoblin Gang, right before its first death spawn of 2 Goblins. This can almost always guarantee some Crown Tower damage if done correctly while delaying the opposing troops from defending the 2 other drills.
  - The Goblin Drill will only resurface when it reaches its health thresholds, but can end up in the second threshold while bypassing the first one. This can happen in scenarios where an attack drops it below 33% when it was at higher than 66%, such as when being hit byLightning.
  - If the user wants to use the Earthquake despite the weaknesses, using a small spell in tandem, such asThe LogorArrows, is highly recommended to take out many of the Goblins before damage can be done to the Crown Tower.
  - The Bowler is the best of these counters as when deployed properly, the lingering attack of the ball will usually destroy both the drill and the Goblins at the same time without any damage being dealt to the Crown Tower. Also, the Bowler will be very healthy afterwards, providing a threatening counterattack.

A card or a feature with a + next to it denotes something that is only available in temporary challenges (has a timer on it, and the event may still come back every once in a while). Merge Tactics cards arepurple, while Evolutions arepink, and Heroes areorange.
