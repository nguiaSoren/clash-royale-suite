The Hero Wizard is aRarecard that is unlocked by unlocking 200 hero fragments and rolling the Hero Wizard at random. The Heroic Wizard takes the appearance of a man who has blonde eyebrows, mustache and goatee, and wears a beige cloak, a team-colored shirt, loincloth and pants, with all of them having a gold outline, a belt with aHero Shardin the middle, two gold bracelets on his arms, and a pair of brown boots, while donning a pair of fiery golden wings. This card costs 5to put on the arena and an additional 1to activate his ability.

## Ability: Fiery Flight[]

The Hero Wizard has a special ability accessible from whatever side of the screen was put in, just above the player'scardslots inbattle. After a 1-second delay, the Hero Wizard activates his fiery flight, which makes him take flight for a few seconds. While he is flying, his fireballs now have their own 3 tile radius tornado, which does its own damage, similar to theEvolved Valkyrie. The ability costs 1to activate.

## Strategy[]

  - Use your Wizard's ability to pull back win conditions such asGolem,Giant, andElixir Golemwhile also taking out supporting units seen with these cards, such asNight Witch,Lumberjack,Witch,Musketeer, andMini P.E.K.K.A.
  - To make the most out of his ability, activate it when he is already attacking enemy troops, so that he does not waste any of his precious ability time moving.
  - The Hero Wizard's ability can be used to dodge the attacks of troops that cannot attack air troops, such as Mini P.E.K.K.A.
