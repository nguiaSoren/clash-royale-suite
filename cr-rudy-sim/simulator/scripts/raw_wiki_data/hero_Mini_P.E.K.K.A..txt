## Release Date

The Heroic Mini P.E.K.K.A. is aRarecardthat is unlocked with 200 hero fragments. It spawns one single-target, ground-targeting, melee, and groundtroopwith high hitpoints and high damage. The Heroic Mini P.E.K.K.A takes the appearance of a robot-looking troop similar toMini P.E.K.K.A.who wears gold metallic armor with two pearl white horns, shoulder and arm pads, golden spikes on his arms and a single round glowing eye, along with a team-colored cape and faulds, while wielding both a cracked refined sword with a pearl white base, a golden hilt and a wooden grip and a golden spatula with a small golden spike near the top. A Heroic Mini P.E.K.K.A. card costs 4to deploy, and his ability costs an additional 1to activate.

## Ability: Breakfast Boost[]

The Heroic Mini P.E.K.K.A. has a special ability accessible from whatever side of the screen was put in, just above the player'scardslots inbattle. After a 1-second delay, the Mini P.E.K.K.A. activates his "Breakfast Boost", immediately eats all the pancakes he cooked, granting him extra Levels and healing himself for 30%.
Heroic Mini P.E.K.K.A. can constantly gain progress for his pancake meters. He will need 22 seconds to get one pancake meter filled, but he can also get 50% progress with every attack. Every filled pancake meter increases the Levels gained when his ability is used by 1, except the last meter grants 2 extra Levels. With no meters, the Heroic Mini P.E.K.K.A. will gain 1 Level, but with a maximum of 3 meters, he can gain 5 Levels. The amount of meters the Heroic Mini P.E.K.K.A. has can be seen in a golden bar above his hitpoint bar. The ability can only be activated once. The Breakfast Boost ability costs 1to activate.

## Strategy[]

  - For the purposes of determiningElixirtrades, the Heroic Mini P.E.K.K.A. and his activated Ability count as 5 Elixir, as the player must play the 4 Elixir Heroic Mini P.E.K.K.A and then spend a further 1 on his Ability.
  - This card would be good in decks with large tanks, as with his ability, he would be able to take out anti-tank cards likeP.E.K.K.A,Mighty Miner,Monk, andPrinceeven better, as well as being good at taking a Tower on its own, while a tank protects him.
  - To counter the Heroic Mini P.E.K.K.A, it is important to stop it before he boosts himself with his Breakfast Boost ability. This includes using theRocketto OHKO him. If the player anticipates the enemy Rocket, they can activate his ability causing him to survive the spell, however this result in its healing being completely wasted.
  - Although he defends the same way as the regular Mini P.E.K.K.A, the Heroic Mini P.E.K.K.A offers a better counterattack afterwards after using his ability, granting him more damage on the counterattack and healing 40% of his hitpoints.
  - When using Heroic Mini P.E.K.K.A, pay attention to your opponent's card rotation. The opponent is more likely to have building-targeting troops to be able to hit your Crown Towers, allowing the Heroic Mini P.E.K.K.A to accumulate his ability easily. Try to reserve the Heroic Mini P.E.K.K.A for that card as much as possible.
  - The Heroic Mini P.E.K.K.A has incredible synergy with theRoyal Chef. The Royal Chef's low damage and lack of burst will force the Heroic Mini P.E.K.K.A to attack more times, accumulating more of his ability.Conversely, he is completely ineffective with theCannoneerandDagger Duchess, as the high damage and quick burst will lower the amount of hits the Heroic Mini P.E.K.K.A will dish out, which will result in less ability meters.
  - Depending on what side of the arena this happens, but if the Mini P.E.K.K.A was facing an equal levelP.E.K.K.A, if the player who played the Mini P.E.K.K.A would have to wait until the P.E.K.K.A strikes, then if all bars are full, then they use the ability right after the first strike from P.E.K.K.A, which will then have a lot more health than theP.E.K.K.Acan take out in 2 or less strikes. By that point though, the Mini P.E.K.K.A would have killed her for a +2 elixir trade.
  - Although situational, the player can manipulate the Royal Chef by using the Heroic Mini P.E.K.K.A to grant an additional Level and gain more hitpoints and damage. This can allow him to accumulate the ability more safely and generate more Levels after using it. The player can further manipulate extra Levels by using Mirror on the Heroic Mini P.E.K.K.A, granting a total of 7 extra Levels on the Heroic Mini P.E.K.K.A that way.
  - Conversely, he is completely ineffective with theCannoneerandDagger Duchess, as the high damage and quick burst will lower the amount of hits the Heroic Mini P.E.K.K.A will dish out, which will result in less ability meters.

## Trivia[]

  - The Mini P.E.K.K.A.'s name derives from the Finnish name for "Peter", which in turn comes from the Greek name "Petros", meaning stone.
  - He is 1 of 3 troops that are “miniature” versions of another troop in Clash of Clans, the others being the Baby Dragon and Electro Dragon. They are a smaller version of P.E.K.K.A., Dragon, and Electro Dragon, respectively.
  - The Heroic Mini P.E.K.K.A. was one of the firstHeroesto be added in the game, alongside theGiant,Knight, andMusketeer.
  - Before the Mini P.E.K.K.A. Hero's release, the Breakfast Boost's healing was decreased to 40% (from 50%).
