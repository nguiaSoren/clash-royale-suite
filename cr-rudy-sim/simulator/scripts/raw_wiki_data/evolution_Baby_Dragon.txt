The Baby Dragon's Evolution is aEpiccardthat is unlocked with 6 Evolution Shards. It spawns a Baby Dragon with identical stats to the original. The Evolved Baby Dragon produces a 8x9 square area of a gust of wind around it that slows enemy troops by 30% and speeds up allies by 30%. After death, the wind will linger for about 2 seconds. The Baby Dragon is a small, spring green dragon with short, stubby, darker team-colored wings, feet and tongue, plum eyes, horns and larger spikes on its wings and back, yellow sclera, and a purple back, and fires a bright, glowing fireball that leaves a purple trail. The Baby Dragon's evolution card costs 4 Elixir to deploy and requires 2 cycle to deploy.

## Strategy[]

  - The Baby Dragon's synergy with theBalloonis even greater. Now, the Evolved Baby Dragon is able to tank for the Balloon and speed it up, allowing it to have a greater chance reaching the opponent's tower.
  - The Evolved Baby Dragon pairs well with building-targeting tanks, such as theGolem,Electro Giant,Elixir Golem,Lava Hound, or theGiant. You can deploy the tank at the back and slowly deploy more troops behind of it, and once they reach the bridge, you can use the Evolved Baby Dragon to speed up all of them.
  - With its slowdown effect, the Evolved Baby Dragon can now defend againstWall Breakers,Skeleton Barrel, andBarbariansby itself, and then be used as a counterpush.It can also stop theBattle Ramand Balloon from reaching the tower, although the Barbarians and the Balloon's death bomb will still damage it.

## History[]

2025
  - On 14/08/2025, the Evolution accidentally appeared in the game but were quickly removed afterwards. It is currently unknown if players who had unlocked the Evolution could still be able to use it.
  - On 1/9/2025, the Baby Dragon Evolution was added to the game on 1/9/2025, in the September 2025 Update.
  - On 6/10/2025, aBalance Updateincreased the cycles required to 2 (from 1). It also fixed a bug where his Deployment Visual Effects would not show.
  - On 1/12/2025, a Balance Update decreased the Baby Dragon's friendly troop speedup by 20%, from 50% down to 30%.
