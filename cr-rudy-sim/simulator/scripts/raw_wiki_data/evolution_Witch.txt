## Release Date

The Witch's Evolution is anEpiccardunlocked with 6 Evolution Shards. It spawns a Witch with identical stats to the original. Once a Skeleton that the Evolved Witch has spawned is defeated, she is healed. This healing can overheal, allowing her to achieve 24% more hitpoints at its maximum capacity. The Evolved Witch takes an appearance as a woman who has glowing pink eyes and violet eyebrows, wears a violet cloak with gold linings, a ram face on top and two horns on the sides and dress, a dark gray top, team-colored armlets, darker purple lipstick, and a belt with theCard Evolutionsymbol in the middle, and wields a staff with a skull on top. The Witch's Evolution costs 5Elixirand requires 1 cycle to deploy.

## Strategy[]

  - Due to the Witch costing only 5Elixirand requiring only 1 cycle to evolve, the player can evolve her multiple times in a match.
  - The Evolved Witch naturally spawnsSkeletonsevery 7 seconds. Due to her slower movement speed than the spawned Skeletons, the Evolved Witch would heal herself once her spawns cross the bridge when played behind theKing's Tower, allowing her to gain an advantage before engaging in fights against enemy units.
  - A total of 3 Skeletons need to be defeated in order to have the Evolved Witch to from base health full overheal hitpoints, which can happen with just her first wave. This increases to total of 14 Skeleton that need to be defeated in order to have the Evolved Witch from 1 hitpoint to full overheal hitpoints, requiring a total of 4 waves, assuming no healing is wasted.
  - Lighterspellsare less effective, as they'll usually defeat the Skeletons spawned around her, which can heal the Evolved Witch enough so that she has a decent amount of hitpoints.Poisonin particular would take out two waves of Skeletons, which heal her even more. Trying to useZapon her spawned Skeletons in order for a troop to break through her is also not very effective, as her health will be healed by their defeat. On the contrary,RocketandLightningare a far more effective way of taking her out, even though they are a -1 Elixir trade, as they will defeat her even if she is already at her maximum hitpoints.
  - Playing her behind slow-moving units, like aGiant,P.E.K.K.A.,Giant Skeleton,Golem, or anElixir Golemwill allow her to support those cards with the spawned Skeletons, while also healing herself from their death, as they also move faster than the slow-moving troop of choice.
  - Although splash units are great at wiping out her spawned Skeletons as well as other troops around her, they will have a harder time in defeating the Evolved Witch herself, since she would heal back with every Skeleton death, while she chips the opposing unit's health down. TheValkyrieis slightly more effective, as her hitpoints are elevated enough to survive the Evolved Witch's attacks.
  - The Evolved Witch can also be played on decks withBalloonandLava Hound. Her possible value will either bait Rocket or Lightning for the Balloon or support the Lava Hound + Balloon push with herself and her Skeletons.

## History[]

2025
  - The Witch's Evolutioncardwas added to the game on 5/5/2025, in theMay 2025 Update.
  - On 6/5/2025, a maintenance break, decreased the Evolved Witch's overheal hitpoints to 124% (from 125%).
  - On 3/6/2025, aBalance Update, decreased the Evolved Witch's healing perSkeletondefeated by 12%.
  - On 8/7/2025, aBalance Update, decreased the Evolved Witch's healing per Skeleton defeated by 12%.
  - On 4/8/2025, theAugust 2025 Updateincreased the Evolved Witch's healing per Skeleton defeated by 36%, and made it so that can only be healed by defeated Skeletons that she spawns instead of any Skeleton. It also made it so that she can no longer be healed by defeatedEvolved SkeletonsorGuards.
  - On 6/10/2025, aBalance Updateincreased the Skeleton's attack time interval to 1.1 seconds (from 1.0 seconds).
