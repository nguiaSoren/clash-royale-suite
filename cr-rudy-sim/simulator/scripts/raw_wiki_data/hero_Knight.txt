The Heroic Knight is aCommoncardthat is unlocked with 200. It spawns one single-target, ground-targeting, melee, and groundtroopwith high hitpoints and moderate damage. The Heroic Knight takes the appearance of a man who has glowing yellow eyes, a bigger blond-colored mustache, goatee and eyebrows, and wears chainmail, a golden chestplate, a team-colored cape, a belt, platinum arm pads with a golden base, and golden shoes with platinum plates and straps on them, while wielding a large saber as his weapon and carrying a large platinum shield with a golden handle, base, mustache and goatee, along with aHeroShard in the middle. A Heroic Knight card costs 3to deploy.

## Ability: Triumphant Taunt[]

The Hero Knight has a special ability accessible from whatever side of the screen was put in, just above the player'scardslots inbattle. After a 1-second delay, the Hero Knight activates his “Triumphant Taunt”, taunting every enemytroopandbuildingin a 7.5-tiles range, causing them to attack him. He also gains a shield. Both the shield and the taunt effect last for 5 seconds, unless the former is destroyed, although enemy troops and buildings will still target him afterwards until he is defeated. The ability goes on a 25 second cooldown after the duration ends. The Triumphant Taunt ability costs 2Elixirto activate.

## Strategy[]

  - For the purposes of determiningElixirtrades, the Heroic Knight and his activated Ability count as 5 Elixir, as the player must play the 3 Elixir Heroic Knight and then spend a further 2 on his Ability.
  - This card would be good inGoblin Barreldecks as with his ability, he will make the tower troop refocus onto himself, letting the Goblins get amazing chip damage on the tower, if left undisturbed by a spell. On the other hand, he can also work well against a Goblin Barrel, pulling the goblins away from the tower with his ability on defense.
  - It would also be good on things that would really need something to tank for it, likeGraveyardandSkeleton Army, preferably with itsEvolution. This with either would be amazing as with Graveyard, Heroic Knight doesn't have to be timed perfectly to work. You just have to place it during the same time period of a few seconds to make it work and not be a waste. Paired with the Evolved Skeleton Army, the Knight can be anywhere on your opponent's side, and until the General is close to dying, then you can use the ability. To help use it further, try to place the Knight at the front, so that it absorbs the damage regardless of the ability, and after taking the first tower, then use it when the ability range is going to affect both the King and Crown Tower left.
  - Spells are completely ineffective against the Heroic Knight. His shield is able to withstand a Fireball of equal Level while the Knight himself will survive any lone spell, including the Rocket. Even spells with multiple hits like the Poison and Void are ineffective against him, with the former even being unable to take out the shield by itself.The Barbarian Barrel and Royal Delivery are slightly more effective against him, due to spawning troops that will continuously attack the Heroic Knight. However, unless he is distracted by something else, the Barbarian and Royal Recruit will not be able to defeat him.
  - Paired with theMini P.E.K.K.A.,Prince, orMighty Mineryou can take down something like aP.E.K.K.A., as with the ability, the P.E.K.K.A will retarget onto the Knight, while either one of the 3 damage dealers finishes her off.
  - Even without the ability, the Heroic Knight can still defend against many things the regular can, but unlike itsEvolution, it can no longer defend better against ranged troops, with its 65% damage reduction when not attacking no longer present.
  - With great timing of the ability, a Heroic Knight can pull away both barrels of anEvolved Goblin Barrelfor a slight negative Elixir trade.
  - If you have a big push coming towards an enemy tower, you can help it succeed by dropping a Heroic Knight on the other lane and activating the ability when defending troops are in range. This is especially useful if the enemy is trying to use a building or ranged troop to defend in the center, because the ability will move the defenders' attention to the other lane, allowing your push to break through. ￼
  - The Barbarian Barrel and Royal Delivery are slightly more effective against him, due to spawning troops that will continuously attack the Heroic Knight. However, unless he is distracted by something else, the Barbarian and Royal Recruit will not be able to defeat him.

## Trivia[]

  - The word "Knight" comes from the German "Knecht", meaning "servant or vassal". Originally, knights were associated with mounted warriors, later being considered a class of lower nobility, until eventually, knighthood had become associated with the ideals ofchivalry, a code of conduct for the perfect courtly Christian warrior. Often, a knight was a vassal who served as an elite fighter or a bodyguard for a lord, with payment in the form of land holdings.
  - The Heroic Knight was one of the firstHeroesto be added in the game, alongside theGiant,Mini P.E.K.K.A., andMusketeer.
  - Before the Knight Hero’s release, the Triumphant Taunt’s Elixir cost was increased to 2 Elixir (from 1 Elixir), but his Shield Hitpoints were increased by 47.27%.
  - He and the Goblins are the only Heroes to be Common card, the rest are Rare cards.
