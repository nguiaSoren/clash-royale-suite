## Release Date

The P.E.K.K.A's Evolution is anEpiccardthat is unlocked with 6 Evolution Shards. It spawns a P.E.K.K.A with identical stats to the original. Once the Evolved P.E.K.K.A takes out atrooporbuilding, a butterfly will spawn around her sword, healing 3.5%-15% of her original hitpoints, the magnitude of the heal being determined by the defeated unit's hitpoints. This healing can overheal, allowing her to achieve 50% more hitpoints at its maximum capacity. She appears as a robot-looking troop larger than theMini P.E.K.K.A.clothed in heavy dark gray armor with gold linings, dark-colored stabilizers and two large magenta spikes on her shoulders, wears team-colored straps with theCard Evolutionsymbol in the center, has magenta eyes and horns, and wields a large, magenta-glowing metal sword with a golden handle. The P.E.K.K.A's Evolution costs 7Elixirand requires 1 cycle to deploy.

## Strategy[]

  - The Evolved P.E.K.K.A's massive damage, healing, and very high hitpoints makes her an incredibly powerful wall on both offense and defense. She is more than capable of shutting down massive ground pushes for a positive Elixir trade, with the trade off of taking heavy damage. Fortunately, she'll heal by destroying troops in the push, keeping her healthy enough to counterattack.
  - Compared to the regularP.E.K.K.A, it isn't recommended to use small to medium ground swarms likeGoblin GangandGuardsto defend against the Evolved P.E.K.K.A. Doing so allows the Evolved P.E.K.K.A to heal back a portion of her health. This will likely leave her healthy enough to attack theCrown Tower, dealing massive damage, if not take out an entire tower.
  - The Evolved P.E.K.K.A should never be ignored in any situation with anytowertroop. Doing so will not just take down a Crown Tower, but also nearly take out theKing's Tower.
  - Along with the regular P.E.K.K.A, the Evolved P.E.K.K.A is one of the only single-card hard counters to theEvolved Mega Knight. Her very high DPS can quickly deplete his hitpoints while her own high hitpoints can easily tank the pushback-jump combo, keeping her healthier for longer. She'll likely deal the finishing blow to him, healing her slightly and providing extra pressure for a neutral Elixir trade.
  - In spite of her healing ability, theEvolved SkeletonsandSkeleton Armycan still counter her thanks to their ability to multiply, along with their sheer number and DPS respectively. They can overwhelm her for long enough until she is defeated. Keep in mind that her healing ability allows her to last a bit longer than a normal P.E.K.K.A, giving the opponent more time to eliminate the Skeletons with a spell likeZaporGiant Snowball.
  - UnlikeEvolved Bats, which recover hitpoints every attack, the Evolved P.E.K.K.A only recovers HP if she defeats a unit. This means that even if the P.E.K.K.A dealt significant damage to a unit, if she doesn't deal the finishing blow; for example, if aDart Goblin,Minions,Spear Goblins,Bats, Skeletons, Guards,Goblins, orArchersfinished off aMega Knight,Giant Skeleton,Elixir Golem,Golem, or aGiant, the P.E.K.K.A was fighting, she won't regain hitpoints.Because of this, when defending against her, it may be a good idea to keep the Evolved P.E.K.K.A busy using a troop with high DPS and high hitpoints, such as aMighty Miner,Monk,Prince,Goblin Machine, orBoss Bandit. This will prevent her from healing while the cheap ground swarm finishes her off.
  - Although being unable to fully counter the Evolved P.E.K.K.A, high DPS cards that can survive at least one hit from her are decently effective stalling options. Due to her slow attack rate, Guards orRoyal Recruits, for instance, can always survive a P.E.K.K.A hit as long as they maintain their shields;Elite Barbarians, while not nearly as cost effective, can deal significant damage to the Evolved P.E.K.K.A while keeping her occupied.
  - Since the Evolved P.E.K.K.A can't attack air troops, high DPS air troops such asMinion Horde,Phoenix, Mega Minion, andInferno Dragonare very effective counters to her. However, supporting cards likeArrows/Evolved ZapandWizard/Musketeer/Electro Wizardrespectively, can neutralize these counters, so deploy them carefully.Although situational, the Evolved P.E.K.K.A can be used as a distraction for enemy air troops, as her hitpoints are high enough to tank many shots from units such asBaby Dragon,Electro Dragon,Skeleton Dragons, orFlying Machine.
  - Long ranged troops, such asMusketeer,Dart Goblin, Flying Machine,Magic Archer,Princess, orEvolved Archerscan work in tandem with a mini tank, likeIce Golem,Valkyrie, orKnight, to fully counter her for a potential positive Elixir trade or counterattack. Their long range prevents the Evolved P.E.K.K.A from healing while the tank kites or distracts her.
  - The Evolved P.E.K.K.A is easily one of the best counters to theEvolved Goblin Giant. Her massive damage per hit allows her to quickly destroy the Goblin Giant himself while the spawnedGoblinsandSpear Goblinswill recover her health significantly, creating a high pressure counterattack.
  - The Evolved P.E.K.K.A can defeatBarbariansup to 3 Levels higher in just 1 hit. This is useful to keep in mind if Barbarians are the opponent's only counter to her.

## History[]

2024
  - The P.E.K.K.A's Evolution was made available on 30/8/2024, before its release date, as an unintended glitch. Supercell fixed this glitch with a maintenance break.
  - The P.E.K.K.A's Evolutioncardwas added to the game on 2/9/2024, in theSeptember 2024 Update.
  - On 8/10/2024, aBalance Update, decreased the Evolved P.E.K.K.A's healing per KO to 10% (from 12.5%).
  - On 14/11/2024, aBalance Update, decreased the Evolved P.E.K.K.A'S healing per KO to 7.5% (from 10%).
  - On 16/12/2024, the2024 Quarter 4 Update, added a sound effect to the Evolved P.E.K.K.A after death.
2025
  - On 8/1/2025, aBalance Update, made it so that Evolved P.E.K.K.A's healing per KO would be based on the target's hitpoints instead of healing the same amount from all troops.
  - On 30/1/2025, amaintenance break, corrected the Evolved P.E.K.K.A's healing per KO value.
  - On 4/2/2025, aBalance Update, decreased the Evolved P.E.K.K.A's range to 1.2 tiles (from 1.6 tiles), now classified as Melee: Medium.
  - On 5/5/2025, theMay 2025 Update, increased the Evolved P.E.K.K.A.'s healing per small unit by 13%, healing per medium unit by 8%, and healing per large unit by 2%.
